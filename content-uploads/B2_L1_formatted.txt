Lesson 1 – Present Simple vs Present Continuous

Exercise 1 – Multiple Choice (10 questions)
Choose the correct option.
1. You are always _____ my car keys, so please check your pockets now. | A. losing  B. lose  C. lost  D. loses | ANSWER: A
2. The late train usually _____ at six tomorrow, but its schedule is often delayed. | A. is leaving  B. leaves  C. is left  D. has left | ANSWER: B
3. John _____ in a café until he finds a better job, so he is quite busy. | A. works  B. work  C. is working  D. has worked | ANSWER: C
4. She _____ in a large office, and she really enjoys the city life. | A. is living temporarily  B. is living  C. lives  D. will live | ANSWER: C
5. I will give you the notes after I _____ the full lecture later today. | A. am finishing  B. finish  C. will finish  D. am going to finish | ANSWER: B
6. He _____ too much coffee these days, but it helps him study for the exams. | A. is drinking  B. drinks  C. always drinks  D. drink | ANSWER: A
7. Water _____ to steam if you heat it to 100 degrees Celsius, and this is always true. | A. is turning  B. turns  C. is turned  D. turn | ANSWER: B
8. Please be quiet now, because I _____ on an important client call at the moment. | A. am talking  B. talk  C. talked  D. have talked | ANSWER: A
9. I _____ in the main building, but I _____ from home this week only. | A. am working / work  B. work / am working  C. am working / am working  D. work / work | ANSWER: B
10. The child is constantly _____ attention, and his parents look exhausted. | A. asks for  B. asked for  C. asking for  D. ask for | ANSWER: C

Exercise 2 – Fill in the Blanks (10 questions)
Fill in the blanks using the correct form of the words from the word bank.
1. Our history teacher _____ us on educational trips every year. | ANSWER: takes
2. This year she _____ us to visit medieval castles. | ANSWER: has been taking
3. She _____ students there for over a decade. | ANSWER: has taken
4. She believes _____ field trips helps us understand history better. | ANSWER: taking
5. Next month, we're planning _____ a special tour of Edinburgh Castle. | ANSWER: to take
6. Sarah _____ her school uniform every day. | ANSWER: wears
7. She _____ it proudly for three years now. | ANSWER: has been wearing
8. Recently, she _____ a badge protesting the strict uniform policy. | ANSWER: has worn
9. She wants _____ more comfortable clothes. | ANSWER: to wear
10. Many students support _____ casual attire instead. | ANSWER: wearing

Exercise 3 – Cloze Test (10 questions)
Choose the best option to complete the passage.
1. Ancient Egyptians _____ that preserving bodies after death was essential for the afterlife journey. | A. are believing  B. believed  C. believe  D. have believed | ANSWER: B
2. The mummification process _____ approximately seventy days and required specialized knowledge. | A. is taking  B. took  C. has taken  D. takes | ANSWER: B
3. Embalmers carefully _____ the internal organs and placed them in canopic jars. | A. removed  B. succeeded in  C. approved of  D. dealt with | ANSWER: A
4. Making mummies _____ a strong understanding of both religious beliefs and practical techniques. | A. called on  B. required  C. depended from  D. insisted in | ANSWER: B
5. _____, archaeologists have discovered thousands of mummies in various Egyptian tombs. | A. Already  B. So far  C. Just  D. Still | ANSWER: B
6. The process typically began _____ the time of death, when priests performed religious ceremonies. | A. in  B. at  C. on  D. by | ANSWER: B
7. _____ the body in natron salt was crucial for removing all moisture and preventing decay. | A. To preserve  B. Preserve  C. Preserved  D. Preserving | ANSWER: D
8. Ancient Egyptians needed _____ the deceased with food, jewelry, and possessions. | A. providing  B. provide  C. provided  D. to provide | ANSWER: D
9. Researchers _____ study these mummies to learn about ancient diseases, diet, and daily life. | A. must  B. should  C. can  D. might | ANSWER: C
10. Scientists have recently _____ important discoveries about Egyptian embalming methods. | A. carried over  B. put up  C. looked into  D. come across | ANSWER: D

Exercise 4 – Choose the Correct Option (8 questions)
Circle the correct word or phrase.
1. Scientists currently study / are studying ancient mummies to discover more about diseases in early Egyptian civilization. | ANSWER: are studying
2. The museum displays / is displaying its famous mummy collection every day, attracting thousands of visitors. | ANSWER: displays
3. Researchers believe / are believing that mummification practices varied depending on social status. | ANSWER: believe
4. The restoration team works / is working on a damaged sarcophagus this week. | ANSWER: is working
5. Ancient Egyptian priests perform / are performing complex rituals during mummification, following strict religious procedures. | ANSWER: perform
6. The laboratory technician analyzes / is analyzing tissue samples from the mummy right now. | ANSWER: is analyzing
7. Archaeologists know / are knowing that preserving bodies required extensive knowledge. | ANSWER: know
8. The documentary film crew records / is recording the entire excavation process today. | ANSWER: is recording
